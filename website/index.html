    Why MiniPets?

    MiniPets is an app that lets you take care of a virtual pet that brings back the nostalgia of Tamagotchi to the present, as well as teaching the younger generations the responsibilities of owning a pet. MiniPets uses a retro 2D, 8-bit art style similar to the one everyone remembers from Tamagotchi (but better).





    Who should download our app?

    Anyone can! It's a great learning tool for parents who's kids can't stop talking about adopting little furry friend, and for people who want to enjoy Tamagotchi once more!





    Fun things to do in our app!



    Play fun minigames like as fetch to earn coins!



    Dress up you furry friend in wacky costumes! Or let them stay naked!

    (No guarantees that you pet will not stalk other pets if naked, like Mr. Freckles here)





    See your pet's reaction after weeks of neglect!



    And so much more!
